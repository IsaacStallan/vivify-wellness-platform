5-Step Career Planning Process

Follow this systematic approach to plan and launch your career successfully:

1

Self-Assessment

Identify your skills, interests, values, and career goals through reflection and assessment tools.

2

Explore Options

Research industries, roles, and career paths that align with your assessment results and interests.

3

Gain Experience

Build relevant experience through internships, volunteering, projects, and part-time work.

4

Develop Skills

Identify skill gaps and pursue learning opportunities, certifications, and professional development.

5

Execute Strategy

Apply for positions, network actively, and continuously refine your approach based on feedback.
