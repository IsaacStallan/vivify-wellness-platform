Your Performance Assessment

This quick assessment helps you understand your current resilience level and identifies areas for growth. Based on your results, you'll unlock personalized training recommendations and habit challenges.

What you'll get:

  • Personalized resilience score and level
  • Unlock achievement badges based on your strengths
  • Custom training recommendations
  • Progress tracking integration with your dashboard
  • Habit challenges tailored to your needs

Note: This is a performance training tool, not a medical assessment. If you're experiencing serious mental health concerns, please reach out to a mental health professional or your school counselor.

Assessment Progress
Question 1 of 20 • ~5 minutes

Physical Foundation

1. How well do you maintain consistent energy levels throughout the day?

2. How would you rate your sleep quality and consistency?

3. How well do you bounce back physically from challenging days?

4. How consistent are you with healthy habits during busy periods?

5. How well do you manage your physical response to pressure?

Mental Toughness

6. How well do you maintain focus during challenging situations?

7. How do you handle setbacks or failures?

8. How well do you manage your thoughts during stressful periods?

9. How confident do you feel about handling future challenges?

10. How well do you maintain perspective during difficult times?

Emotional Regulation

11. How well do you manage your emotions under pressure?

12. How quickly do you recover from disappointment or frustration?

13. How well do you maintain motivation during tough periods?

14. How do you handle criticism or feedback?

15. How well do you practice self-compassion when you make mistakes?

Social Support & Connection

16. How strong is your support network when you need help?

17. How comfortable are you asking for help when you need it?

18. How well do you maintain relationships during stressful periods?

19. How well do you communicate your needs to others?

20. How well do you support others in your community?
