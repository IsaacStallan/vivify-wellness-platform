Format

Online via Zoom (link provided)

Cost

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any special equipment or software?
Just a device with internet access and Zoom installed (it's free). We recommend using headphones for better audio quality, but it's not essential.
What if I'm too shy to participate?
That's completely fine! You can join with your camera off and just listen. Many students start this way and gradually become more comfortable participating. There's no pressure to share anything personal.
Can my parents attend with me?
These workshops are designed specifically for students and work best when it's just young people. However, we do run occasional parent information sessions about supporting teenage Focus & Resilience.
What if I miss a session I've registered for?
No worries! Since each workshop runs weekly, you can join the next session. We'll also send you a summary of what was covered and any resources shared.
Are the workshops really free?
Yes, completely free! Vivify is committed to making Focus & Resilience resources accessible to all Australian students, regardless of their family's financial situation.
Will my information be kept private?
Absolutely. We follow strict privacy guidelines. What's shared in workshops stays in workshops, and we only collect the minimum information needed for registration.
