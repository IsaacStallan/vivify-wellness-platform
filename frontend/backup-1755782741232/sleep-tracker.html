Sleep Tips for Students

Consistent Schedule
Try to go to bed and wake up at the same time every day, even on weekends. This helps regulate your body's internal clock.
Screen Time Limits
Avoid screens for at least 1 hour before bedtime. Blue light can interfere with your natural sleep cycle.
Caffeine Cutoff
Avoid caffeine after 2 PM. It can stay in your system for 6-8 hours and disrupt your ability to fall asleep.
Sleep Environment
Keep your room cool, dark, and quiet. Consider blackout curtains or a sleep mask for better sleep quality.
Exercise Regularly
Regular physical activity can help you fall asleep faster and enjoy deeper sleep, but avoid vigorous exercise close to bedtime.
Manage Stress
Practice relaxation techniques like deep breathing, meditation, or journaling before bed to help calm your mind.
