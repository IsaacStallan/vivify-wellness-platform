Explore Meditations

Meditation is a powerful practice that can help you navigate the challenges of student life. Our guided meditations are designed to fit into your busy schedule with options ranging from quick 5-minute exercises to deeper 30-minute sessions. Whether you're new to meditation or have an established practice, these resources will help you cultivate mindfulness and reduce stress.

Benefits of Meditation for Students

Improved Focus

Regular meditation enhances concentration and helps sustain attention, making studying more effective.

Stress Reduction

Meditation activates your relaxation response, lowering stress hormones and easing anxiety.

Better Sleep

Calming meditation practices can help quiet the mind and improve sleep quality and duration.

Emotional Regulation

Develop greater awareness of emotions and learn to respond rather than react to challenging situations.

Beginner's Guide to Meditation

New to meditation? Follow these simple steps to establish a sustainable practice:

1

Start Small

Begin with just 5 minutes daily. It's better to meditate consistently for a short time than irregularly for longer periods. Try our "5-Minute Breathing Reset" as your first meditation.

2

Create a Routine

Choose a specific time each day for your practice. Many students find that meditating first thing in the morning or right before bed works best. Link meditation to an existing habit to make it easier to remember.

3

Find a Quiet Space

Designate a specific place for meditation, ideally somewhere quiet where you won't be disturbed. This could be a corner of your dorm room or a quiet spot in the library.

4

Be Patient with Yourself

It's normal for your mind to wander during meditation. When you notice this happening, gently bring your attention back to your meditation without judgment. This is part of the practice.

5

Track Your Progress

Notice how meditation affects your stress levels, focus, and sleep quality over time. Use the Meditation Tracker in your Vivify Dashboard to log your practice and see your progress.
