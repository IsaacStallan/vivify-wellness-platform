Become a Mentor

Share your knowledge and experiences to help fellow students thrive. Join our community of mentors making a real difference in students' lives while developing your own leadership and communication skills.

Apply to beome a Mentor

Why Become a Mentor?

Make a Real Impact

Help fellow students overcome challenges you've faced. Your guidance can be the difference between someone struggling and thriving in their academic journey.

Develop Leadership Skills

Enhance your communication, empathy, and leadership abilities. These skills are invaluable for your future career and personal growth.

Build Your Network

Connect with students from different backgrounds and disciplines. Expand your network while building meaningful relationships.

Gain Recognition

Receive official recognition for your mentoring contributions. Add this valuable experience to your resume and LinkedIn profile.

Learn from Teaching

Reinforce your own knowledge by teaching others. Gain new perspectives and insights from your mentees' fresh viewpoints.

Personal Satisfaction

Experience the joy of seeing others succeed because of your support. There's nothing quite like helping someone achieve their goals.

Mentor Requirements

We're looking for passionate students who want to make a difference. Here's what we're looking for:

Academic Standing

Currently enrolled as a 2nd year or higher student with good academic standing (GPA 3.0+)

Time Commitment

Ability to dedicate 2-4 hours per week to mentoring activities and regular check-ins with mentees

Communication Skills

Strong interpersonal and communication skills with the ability to listen actively and provide constructive feedback

Mentoring Experience

Previous mentoring, tutoring, or leadership experience preferred but not required. Passion to help others is essential!

Mentor Training Program

We provide comprehensive training to ensure you're equipped with the skills and knowledge to be an effective mentor.

Building Rapport
Learn how to establish trust and create meaningful connections with your mentees
Active Listening
Develop skills to truly understand your mentees' needs and concerns
Goal Setting
Help mentees set realistic, achievable goals and track their progress
Boundaries & Ethics
Understand professional boundaries and ethical considerations in mentoring
Crisis Support
Recognize warning signs and know when to refer mentees to professional help
Tracking Progress
Learn tools and techniques for monitoring mentee development and success

After submitting your application, our team will review it within 3-5 business days. If selected, you'll be invited to a brief video interview and then enrolled in our mentor training program. The entire process typically takes 1-2 weeks from application to active mentoring!
