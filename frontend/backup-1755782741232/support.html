How do I create an account? +

To create an account, click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of any page. Fill out the registration form with your email address, create a username and password, and accept the terms of service. You'll receive a verification email to confirm your account. Click the verification link, and you're all set!

I forgot my password. How do I reset it? +

Click on the "Login" button, then select "Forgot Password?" You'll be prompted to enter the email address associated with your account. We'll send you an email with a secure link to reset your password. If you don't receive the email within a few minutes, check your spam folder or contact support for assistance.

How do I update my profile information? +

Once logged in, click on your profile icon in the top right corner, then select "Profile" from the dropdown menu. On your profile page, click the "Edit Profile" button to update your personal information, change your profile picture, adjust privacy settings, or update your performance goals.

How do I delete my account? +

To delete your account, go to your Profile settings, scroll to the bottom, and click "Account Management." Select "Delete Account" and follow the confirmation process. Please note that account deletion is permanent and will remove all your data from our system. If you have any concerns before deleting your account, please contact our support team.

Does Vivify work on mobile devices? +

Yes, Vivify is fully responsive and works on all modern mobile devices. Our web application automatically adjusts to your screen size, whether you're using a smartphone, tablet, or desktop computer. For the best experience, we recommend using the latest version of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge.

Why won't the website load properly? +

If you're experiencing loading issues, try these troubleshooting steps:
1. Clear your browser cache and cookies
2. Ensure you're using a supported browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
3. Check your internet connection
4. Disable browser extensions that might interfere with the site
5. Try accessing the site in incognito/private browsing mode

If problems persist, please contact our technical support with details about your device and browser.

How do I enable notifications? +

To enable browser notifications for Vivify:
1. Go to your Profile settings
2. Select "Notifications"
3. Toggle on the notifications you'd like to receive
4. When prompted by your browser to allow notifications, click "Allow"

You can customize which types of notifications you receive, including performance reminders, new content alerts, and community updates.

Why can't I upload a profile picture? +

If you're having trouble uploading a profile picture, please ensure:
1. The file is in JPG, PNG, or GIF format
2. The file size is under 5MB
3. You have a stable internet connection during upload

If you continue to experience issues, try using a different browser or device, or contact our support team for assistance.

How often is new content added? +

We add new content weekly across all four performance pillars: Fitness, Nutrition, Focus & Resilience, and Life Skills. Major content updates typically happen at the beginning of each semester to align with the academic calendar. You can subscribe to content update notifications in your profile settings to be alerted when new resources relevant to your interests are added.

Can I suggest new content topics? +

Absolutely! We welcome content suggestions from our community. You can submit your ideas through:
1. The "Suggest Content" form in the feedback section of your dashboard
2. Our contact page (select "Content Suggestion" from the subject dropdown)
3. Directly emailing content@vivify.edu with your ideas

Our content team reviews all suggestions and prioritizes topics based on student demand and relevance.

Are there advanced fitness programs available? +

Yes, we offer fitness programs for all levels, including advanced options. After completing your fitness assessment, you'll receive recommendations tailored to your experience level. Advanced programs include progressive overload strength training, high-intensity interval training (HIIT), specialized sport-specific training, and more. You can find these in the Fitness section under "Advanced Programs."

How do I save or bookmark content for later? +

To save content for later, look for the bookmark icon (🔖) in the top right corner of any article, video, or resource page. Clicking this icon will add the item to your "Saved Content" collection, which you can access from your dashboard or profile. You can organize saved content into custom collections (e.g., "Exam Prep Stress Relief," "Quick Workouts") for easier access later.
