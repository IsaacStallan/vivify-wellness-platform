Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in accordance with Australian privacy laws.

Australian Privacy Compliance

Vivify is committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). As an Australian service designed for students, we maintain the highest standards of data protection and transparency.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you use Vivify, we may collect the following types of personal information:

Information Type Examples Purpose
Account Information Name, email address, username, password Account creation and authentication
Educational Information School/university name, year level, course of study Personalised content and recommendations
performance Data Fitness goals, nutrition preferences, Focus & Resilience check-ins Tracking progress and providing tailored support
Financial Information Budgeting goals, expense categories (no banking details) Educational financial planning tools
Usage Information Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform Improving user experience and platform functionality
Device Information Browser type, operating system, IP address Security, platform optimisation, and fraud prevention

Information We Don't Collect

We never collect: Banking details, credit card information, student ID numbers, Tax File Numbers (TFN), Medicare numbers, or any other sensitive government identifiers. Our financial tools are educational only.

How We Collect Information

  • Directly from you: When you create an account, complete your profile, or use our performance tracking tools
  • Automatically: Through cookies and similar technologies when you use our platform
  • From third parties: Only with your explicit consent, such as integration with university systems or health apps

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes, all of which support our mission to improve student wellbeing:

Core Platform Functions

  • Provide personalised performance recommendations and content
  • Track your progress across fitness, nutrition, Focus & Resilience, and life skills
  • Enable community features and peer support connections
  • Deliver educational content relevant to Australian students
  • Provide customer support and respond to your enquiries

Platform Improvement

  • Analyse usage patterns to improve our services
  • Develop new features based on user needs and feedback
  • Conduct research to better understand student performance challenges
  • Ensure platform security and prevent misuse

Communication

  • Send important platform updates and security notifications
  • Provide performance reminders and encouragement (with your consent)
  • Share relevant resources and educational content
  • Respond to your support requests and feedback

Marketing Communications: We will only send you marketing communications if you explicitly opt-in. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email or by contacting us directly.

When We Share Your Information

Your privacy is paramount. We follow strict guidelines about when and how we share your information:

We May Share Information When:

  • You give explicit consent: For example, participating in research studies or connecting with peer support groups
  • Required by Australian law: To comply with legal obligations, court orders, or regulatory requirements
  • Emergency situations: If we believe disclosure is necessary to prevent serious harm to you or others
  • Service providers: With trusted third-party providers who help us operate the platform (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Business transfers: In the unlikely event of a merger or acquisition (with notification to all users)

We Never Share:

  • Your personal performance data for commercial purposes
  • Individual health information with universities or educational institutions
  • Your information with advertisers for targeted marketing
  • Detailed personal information with other users without your consent

Anonymous and Aggregated Data

We may share anonymous, aggregated data for research purposes to help improve student performance programs across Australia. This data cannot be linked back to any individual user.

How We Protect Your Data

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information:

Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest using industry-standard encryption
  • Secure hosting: Our servers are hosted in Australia with enterprise-grade security measures
  • Access controls: Strict authentication and authorisation protocols for all team members
  • Regular security audits: Ongoing vulnerability assessments and penetration testing
  • Automated backups: Regular, secure backups to prevent data loss

Operational Safeguards

  • Staff training: All team members receive privacy and security training
  • Limited access: Information is only accessed by authorised personnel for legitimate purposes
  • Incident response: Comprehensive procedures for handling any security incidents
  • Data retention policies: Clear guidelines on how long we keep different types of information

Australian Data Hosting

Your data stays in Australia: All personal information is stored on servers located within Australia, ensuring compliance with local privacy laws and faster access for Australian users.

Your Privacy Rights

Under Australian privacy law, you have important rights regarding your personal information:

Right to Access

Request a copy of all personal information we hold about you, including how we use it and who we share it with.

Right to Correction

Ask us to correct any personal information that is inaccurate, out-of-date, incomplete, or misleading.

Right to Deletion

Request deletion of your personal information in certain circumstances, such as when it's no longer needed.

Data Portability

Request your personal information in a portable format to transfer to another service provider.

Right to Object

Object to certain uses of your personal information, particularly for direct marketing purposes.

Right to Complain

Lodge a complaint with us or the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC).

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in the Contact Us section. We will respond to your request within 30 days. There is no charge for reasonable requests, but we may charge a fee for excessive or repetitive requests.

Identity Verification

To protect your privacy, we may need to verify your identity before responding to requests about your personal information. We'll ask for the minimum information necessary to confirm your identity.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience on Vivify:

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Enable core platform functionality, security, and user authentication Session or until logout
Preference Cookies Remember your settings and personalise your experience Up to 1 year
Analytics Cookies Help us understand how you use the platform to improve our services Up to 2 years
Performance Cookies Monitor platform performance and identify technical issues Up to 6 months

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Block all cookies (may limit platform functionality)
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Set preferences for different types of cookies
  • Receive notifications before cookies are set

Note: Essential cookies are necessary for the platform to function properly. Disabling these may prevent you from using certain features or accessing your account.

Student-Specific Privacy Protections

As a platform designed for Australian students, we provide additional privacy protections:

Under-18 Users

  • We require parental consent for users under 18 years of age
  • Additional safeguards are in place for collecting and using information from minors
  • Parents can request access to or deletion of their child's information
  • We do not knowingly market to children under 13

University Partnerships

  • We do not share individual student data with educational institutions without explicit consent
  • Any research partnerships are conducted with aggregated, anonymous data only
  • Students maintain full control over their personal information regardless of university affiliation

Focus & Resilience Information

  • Focus & Resilience tracking data receives enhanced protection
  • We provide clear information about mandatory reporting requirements in emergency situations
  • Access to Focus & Resilience professionals and support services is facilitated while maintaining privacy

Financial Education Data

  • Budgeting and financial planning data is kept strictly confidential
  • We do not share financial information with banks, creditors, or financial institutions
  • All financial tools are educational and do not require access to actual bank accounts

Data Retention and Deletion

How Long We Keep Your Information

Information Type Retention Period Reason
Account Information Until account deletion + 30 days Account functionality and security
performance Data Until account deletion or user request Progress tracking and personalisation
Usage Analytics Maximum 3 years Platform improvement and research
Support Communications 3 years after resolution Quality assurance and legal compliance
Financial/Legal Records 7 years Australian legal and tax requirements

Automatic Deletion

We automatically delete:

  • Inactive accounts after 2 years of no activity (with prior notification)
  • Temporary data and logs according to our data retention schedule
  • Marketing communications data when you unsubscribe
  • Session data when you log out or your session expires

International Data Transfers

We are committed to keeping your data within Australia. However, in limited circumstances, we may need to transfer data overseas:

When International Transfers Occur

  • Using cloud services with international infrastructure (with data residency controls)
  • Accessing specialist technical support services
  • Collaborating on research with international academic institutions (anonymous data only)
  • Complying with legal requests from foreign courts (with appropriate legal review)

Safeguards for International Transfers

  • All transfers comply with Australian Privacy Principles
  • Contractual protections requiring equivalent privacy standards
  • Encryption of all data in transit and at rest
  • Regular audits of international service providers
  • Notification to users when data leaves Australia (where possible)

Third-Party Services and Integrations

Vivify may integrate with third-party services to enhance your experience. We carefully select partners who meet our privacy standards:

Types of Third-Party Services

  • Analytics Services: To understand platform usage and improve user experience
  • Communication Tools: For customer support and notifications
  • Security Services: To protect against fraud and cyber threats
  • Educational Integrations: With university systems (with your explicit consent)
  • Health and Fitness Apps: Optional integrations to track performance data

Third-Party Privacy Policies

When you use third-party services through Vivify, their privacy policies may also apply. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any third-party services you choose to use.

Your Control: All third-party integrations are optional. You can disconnect any third-party service from your Vivify account at any time through your account settings.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors.

How We Notify You of Changes

  • Major Changes: We will notify you via email and prominent notice on the platform at least 30 days before changes take effect
  • Minor Changes: We will update the "Last Updated" date and notify you through the platform
  • Legal Requirements: Some changes may be effective immediately if required by law

Your Options

If you don't agree with changes to this Privacy Policy, you can:

  • Contact us to discuss your concerns
  • Adjust your privacy settings to limit data collection
  • Delete your account if you no longer wish to use our services
