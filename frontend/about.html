Our Story

From Knox Grammar classrooms to a performance training platform

Vivify started with a simple observation: the most successful students weren't just smarter—they had better systems. They trained their minds and bodies like athletes train for competition.

After conversations with students at Knox Grammar School, we realized there was a gap. Students wanted to improve but didn't have a structured way to build the daily habits that drive performance. They needed a system that made excellence measurable and achievable.

So we built Vivify—not as a therapy app, but as a performance training platform. Think of it as Strava for your entire life: track habits, compete in challenges, and build the routines that elite performers use every day.

We're focused on one thing: helping ambitious Australian students develop championship habits during their most formative years.

What We Stand For

The principles that guide everything we build

Performance Over Perfection

We focus on consistent progress, not impossible standards. Small daily improvements compound into extraordinary results.

Student-Centered Design

Built by listening to real students. Every feature is designed to solve actual problems faced by Australian secondary school students.

Privacy & Safety First

Your data is yours. We never sell student information, and parents can always review what their children are tracking.

Make It Engaging

Building habits shouldn't feel like homework. We use gamification, challenges, and competition to make self-improvement actually enjoyable.

Evidence-Based

We track what matters. Every metric we measure has been shown to correlate with academic performance, physical health, or mental resilience.

Community Support

You're not alone. Connect with other students building the same habits, share strategies, and motivate each other.

How Vivify Works

A systematic approach to building championship habits

01

Baseline Assessment

Understand where you are across four key areas: physical performance, mental focus, smart nutrition, and life skills. No judgment, just data.

02

Daily Habit Tracking

Choose from proven habits or create your own. Track progress daily and watch your performance scores improve as consistency builds.

03

Structured Challenges

Join 7-30 day challenges that push you to new levels. From morning routines to focus sprints, compete with peers and unlock achievements.

04

Community Leaderboards

See how you stack up. Compete on weekly, monthly, and all-time leaderboards. Learn from top performers and motivate each other.
