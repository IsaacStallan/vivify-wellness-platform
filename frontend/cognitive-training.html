Cognitive Training Tips

1

Consistent Practice

Train for 15-20 minutes daily rather than long infrequent sessions. Consistency builds stronger neural pathways than intensity.

2

Progressive Challenge

Gradually increase difficulty as you improve. The sweet spot is 80% success rate - challenging but not frustrating.

3

Diverse Training

Rotate between different cognitive domains. Train memory, attention, processing speed, and flexibility for comprehensive improvement.

4

Focus Environment

Train in a quiet, distraction-free environment. Your brain needs to concentrate fully to build stronger cognitive abilities.

5

Rest and Recovery

Take breaks between training sessions. Your brain consolidates learning during rest periods, making gains permanent.

6

Real-World Transfer

Practice applying cognitive skills to academic tasks. Connect training improvements to real studying and problem-solving.
