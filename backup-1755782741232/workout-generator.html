Your Personalized Workout Plan

Customized based on your goals, experience, and preferences

Primary Goal: Build Strength

Frequency: 3 days per week

Duration: 30-45 minutes per session

Equipment: Minimal equipment needed

Intensity: Moderate

Weekly Schedule

Day 1: Full Body Strength

  • Goblet Squats

    3 sets of 12 reps

    Focus on depth and maintaining an upright chest.

  • Push-ups

    3 sets of 10-12 reps

    Modify with knee push-ups if needed for proper form.

  • Dumbbell Rows

    3 sets of 12 reps per arm

    Keep your back flat and core engaged throughout the movement.

  • Glute Bridges

    3 sets of 15 reps

    Focus on squeezing glutes at the top of the movement.

  • Plank

    3 sets of 30-45 second holds

    Keep your body in a straight line from head to heels.

Day 2: Cardio & Core

  • Mountain Climbers

    3 sets of 40 seconds work, 20 seconds rest

    Maintain a steady pace with good form.

  • Russian Twists

    3 sets of 20 reps (10 per side)

    Add weight for increased difficulty if needed.

  • High Knees

    3 sets of 40 seconds work, 20 seconds rest

    Focus on height and driving with your arms.

  • Bicycle Crunches

    3 sets of 20 reps (10 per side)

    Focus on quality over speed.

  • Jumping Jacks

    3 sets of 40 seconds work, 20 seconds rest

    Full range of motion with each rep.

Day 3: Upper Body Focus

  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press

    3 sets of 10-12 reps

    Control the weight on the way down.

  • Bodyweight Rows

    3 sets of 12 reps

    Use a table or desk if you don't have a bar.

  • Dumbbell Chest Press

    3 sets of 10-12 reps

    Keep elbows at a 45-degree angle to your body.

  • Tricep Dips

    3 sets of 12-15 reps

    Use a chair or sturdy desk.

  • Arm Circles

    2 sets of 30 seconds forward, 30 seconds backward

    Good for shoulders and cooling down.

Workout Notes & Tips

  • Start each workout with a 5-minute warm-up (light cardio and dynamic stretching).
  • End each workout with a 5-minute cool-down (static stretching).
  • Rest 60-90 seconds between sets for strength exercises.
  • For best results, maintain consistent workout frequency.
  • Increase weights or reps gradually as exercises become easier.
  • Stay hydrated before, during, and after workouts.
  • Focus on proper form rather than lifting heavier weights.
  • Listen to your body and adjust as needed.
