Crisis Resources

If you're experiencing a mental health crisis or need immediate support, please reach out:

  • Lifeline Australia: 13 11 14 - 24/7 crisis support and suicide prevention
  • Kids Helpline (Under 25): 1800 55 1800 - Free 24/7 counselling
  • Beyond Blue: 1300 22 4636 - Depression and anxiety support
  • Emergency Services: 000 - For immediate danger or medical emergency

Remember: Seeking help is a sign of strength, not weakness. You don't have to face challenges alone.
